Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Description

Finally, the PERFECT soup for the fall and winter season, lets get started!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of tomatos
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped into big chunks
  • 1 shallot, peeled and chopped into big chunks
  • 1 head garlic, cloves separated but left peeled, lightly smashed
  • 1 large carrot, roughly chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Shaved Parmigiano Reggiano

Directions

  1. Lightly roast vegetables in a 400 degreeoven for 15 minutes
  2. Remove vegetables from oven and place into a blender with fresh basil. Blend vegetables until mostly smooth (I like to leave them a little chunky)
  3. Pour into pot and heat over medium heat. Add in your heavy cream, salt, and pepper
  4. Heat and Enjoy!
